About the Artist

"I can never see a day in my life that Art will not be a part of."

- Matty Rivera

Matty Rivera was born in Mexico City, at the age of six she moved to Toronto, Canada. From a young age Matty always dreamed of being an artist, and that dream was closer to reality when she was accepted to the Cyber Arts program at Don Mills Collegiate Institute.

From high school, Matty went on to attend Ontario College of Art and Design where she is currently working towards getting her Bachelors of Design. From oils to illustrator, Matty has been able to adapt to almost any medium, but her most natural medium. Aside from art Matty also has a love for animals, especially her own little grey lion Napoleon.
